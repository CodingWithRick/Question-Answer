2. What is the goal of the game?

Ans - The player has to control the paddle using the left and right arrow keys and prevent the ball from falling and destroy all the bricks using that ball.

3. Write a brief story of your game?

Ans - Every time during winter, Harris's locality holds many competitions. Of these, balancing a ball on a bat is Harris's favorite. During this pandemic, since the neighborhood could not hold any competition, Harris is extremely disappointed. So his mother asked me to create a game for him that could be played at home and could bring back the smile on Harris's face. So I created this game Brick Breaker where Harris has to balance the ball on the paddle. He can move the paddle using the left and right arrow keys to save the ball and break the bricks. He has three lives in all within which he has to break all the bricks. As soon as the ball touches any brick, it will turn into a fireball and increase the score. If the ball falls, then Harris's life will be deducted. If Harris manages to break all the bricks within the three lives, he wins or else he loses.

4. Which are the playing characters of this game?

Ans - This is a single player game and the playing character is the paddle. This paddle is controlled by the users using the left and right arrow keys.

5. Which are the Non Playing Characters of this game?

Ans - The NPC or Non Playing Characters are the bricks and the ball. The ball has friction and bounces off the paddle and all the edges except the bottom edge. The bricks are static in position and have random colors. As soon as the ball touches the bricks, they are destroyed and the score increases by 5.

6. What is the device that your player will play on?

Ans - The player can play on all devices like computers, laptop, tablets and mobile phones. It is completely responsive.
